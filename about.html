who we are

WD Rascals joins us from Paris. The band has a light sound and golden style, bringing eclectic guitars, jilted rhythm, extreme vocals, and turquoise songwriting to the table. This band has been traversing ears in venues around the world since 1997.WD Rascals plays tunes from a variety of genres, and their influences include bands such as "Wham!" and "the Killers". Look out for WD Rascals coming to a ski lodge near you this mud season!
